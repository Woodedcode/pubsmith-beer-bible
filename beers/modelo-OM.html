Beer Info

Modelo Especial may be relatively light in color, but this pilsner-style lager
is anything but dull or watery. A delicate, herbaceous hop profile gives
way to a richer palate with notes of honey, subtle malt,
and maize, buoyed by lively carbonation and punctuated with a crisp finish.
