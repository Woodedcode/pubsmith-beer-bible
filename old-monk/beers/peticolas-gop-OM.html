Beer Info

In the spirit of a true session, Golden Opportunity is modeled after the classic styles
of Koln, Germany, but which deliberately lacks the enhanced fruitiness of the typical American
interpretation. Straw-like in color with a dense white head, this medium-bodied brew has a
light, pleasant malt aroma with a hint of sweetness and faint bitterness. It is clean,
well-balanced and suitably carbonated for a crisp, refreshing feel.

Type of beer

Kolsch
